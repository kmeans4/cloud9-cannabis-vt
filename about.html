ABOUT US

CARL MEANS

FOUNDER

Welcome to Cloud 9 Canna, where a humble bedroom endeavor has blossomed into a passion-driven journey. I'm Carl Means, and my cannabis story is one of growth, both in terms of plants and personal evolution. From those early days nurturing cannabis in my bedroom to mastering the art of cultivation at a sprawling 60,000-plant organic CBD farm, I've honed my skills, cultivated a deep understanding of the plant, and embraced my true calling – producing small-batch, top-tier cannabis.

What sets our cannabis apart is the unwavering commitment to excellence and the pursuit of perfection. Each plant we cultivate benefits from the knowledge and experience I've gained over the years. We take pride in our meticulous attention to detail, sustainable practices, and our dedication to delivering a cannabis experience that exceeds your highest expectations.

Here at Cloud 9 Canna, we're not just cultivating plants; we're crafting moments, elevating experiences, and sharing our passion for premium cannabis with the world. Join us on this journey, explore our carefully curated strains, and discover the essence of cannabis excellence. Welcome to a world of small-batch, top-of-the-line cannabis, where quality and dedication are our guiding principles.
