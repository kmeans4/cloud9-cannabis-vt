Mule Fuel
GMO Cookies x Lurch

Mule Fuel is a heavily indica-dominant hybrid strain created through crossing the classic GMO (Garlic Mushroom Onion) Cookies with Lurch. Best known for its full-bodied, sedating high, this strain delivers a powerful experience that starts with a cerebral lift before melting into deep physical relaxation. The aroma is bold and complex - heavy diesel and gas upfront with savory garlic, mushroom, and onion notes from its GMO parentage, layered with earthy musk and hints of chemical funk. This award-winning strain from Thug Pug Genetics produces frosty, trichome-capped buds with dark green-purple coloring and short orange pistils. Mule Fuel is best enjoyed when you're ready to unwind and sink into the couch.

Golden Goat
Hawaiian-Romulan x Island Sweet Skunk

Golden Goat was created by accident in Topeka, Kansas when a male Hawaiian-Romulan pollinated Mr. Dank's Island Sweet Skunk. This sativa-dominant hybrid delivers an uplifting, energetic high that sparks creativity and euphoria. The aroma is unmistakably pungent — a sweet and sour blend with tropical citrus notes, lime zest, and hints of sage. Flavors of tropical fruit, sweet citrus, and earthy undertones dance on the palate. Known for its bright, golden-amber coloring and dense, resinous buds, Golden Goat provides full-body effects with a delightful rush of happiness and mental clarity. Perfect for daytime use when you need a mood lift and creative boost.

Blueberry Pie
‍
Blueberry Gushers x Georgia Pie

Blueberry Pie is an indica-dominant hybrid that combines the gassy sweetness of Blueberry Gushers with the creamy Kush profile of Georgia Pie. This strain delivers a robust flavor experience of sweet berries, vanilla, and nuts layered with gas, cream, and gelato Kush undertones. The aroma is pungent and dessert-like with hints of spiciness. Expect euphoric, relaxing effects that provide tactile body relief while maintaining mood-forward clarity. With frost-coated flowers and a deeply indulgent terpene profile, Blueberry Pie offers a pastry-and-compote experience that's perfect for unwinding while staying pleasantly alert.

Gas Light
Blue Petrol x Blue Petrol

Gas Light is a potent hybrid from Sin City Seeds, created by crossing Blue Petrol with itself. This strain delivers balanced effects that kick your focus, creativity, and mood into high gear without the couch lock. True to its name, Gas Light features a gassy, funky aroma with sweet undertones and a pungent, fuel-forward terpene profile. The buds are tall with frost-coated flowers and high yields. Expect an uplifting cerebral buzz that enhances creativity and focus, followed by smooth body relaxation that keeps you functional and productive. Perfect for daytime use when you need to stay sharp while enjoying a pleasant, mood-elevating experience.

Randy's Candy
‍
Crack Berry Zkittlez x Hey You

Randy's Candy by Goonie Genetics is a unique hybrid that blends intricate genetics from ruderalis, indica, and sativa plants. Created by crossing Crack Berry Zkittlez with Hey You, this strain delivers a mellow, chill vibe that sparks creativity while keeping you uplifted and motivated. The flavor profile is complex and dessert-like, featuring notes of licorice, anise, clove, fig, and candied plum. The aroma is sweet and candy-forward with spicy undertones. With a flowering time of 60-65 days, Randy's Candy produces beautiful buds that offer a balanced, feel-good experience perfect for any time of day when you want to stay positive and creatively inspired.
